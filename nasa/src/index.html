Getting to Know our Space Stations and Boarding Procedures
  1. Make sure you read and follow all the instrucions attached to your ticket.
  2. Please note that attending our induction sessions is absolutely vital and failing to do so could be resulted in your flight's cancellation.
  3. To familiarise yourself with the space craft, boarding two weeks prior to the date of departure is highly recommended.
  4. Medical insurance is included in your ticket. So, you don't need to worry about any treatments you might need. However, if you have any concerns please do not hesitate to contact us.
